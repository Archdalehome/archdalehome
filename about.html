About Us

Archdale is a quiet town located in Highpoint, North Carolina, USA. Thanks to its unique geographical location as the "World Furniture Capital", it boasts a century-old history of furniture design and production, as well as experienced furniture artisans. Archdale Home was born here, specializing in the design and production of leisure home products.

The headquarters of Archdale Home in the United States originated from the love of life and was inspired by the natural beauty and charm of North Carolina. Our furniture reflects the warmth and enthusiasm of life in the South. Each piece is meticulously crafted by skilled artisans who take pride in their artistry and attention to detail.

We only use the highest quality materials and adopt sustainable development practices to ensure that our furniture is not only beautiful but also environmentally friendly. From the external fabric to the internal filling, each component has been carefully selected to stand the test of time and provide lasting comfort and durability. In 2015, we introduced the brand to the Chinese region for sales. With its unique design, exquisite quality and perfect after-sales service, it has won the love of many young consumers.
